Who I Am

I'm Samuel Victor Toochukwu, a self-taught creative and tech enthusiast with a passion for turning ideas into visuals and experiences.
I specialize in graphics design, web development, and video editing — and I'm currently diving deeper into UI/UX design to expand how I solve user-centered problems.
What started as a hobby quickly grew into a calling. From designing flyers for churches to building simple websites, I’ve seen how design and tech can truly transform how people interact, promote, and grow their work.
I enjoy combining creativity with functionality, always aiming to deliver clean, impactful, and intentional results.

I’m based in Onitsha Anambra State, and when I’m not designing or coding, I’m usually learning something new, experimenting with tools, or helping others get their ideas off the ground.
I believe every project is a chance to grow and serve, and I’m excited to keep building, designing, and growing with people and brands that value creativity and innovation.
